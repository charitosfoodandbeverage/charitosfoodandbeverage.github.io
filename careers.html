Kitchen Staff / Cook

Full-Time On-Site Talon-Talon, ZC

We're looking for a reliable and skilled kitchen team member to help prepare our signature dishes — Murtabak, Roti, Burgers, and more. No prior formal kitchen experience required; we'll train you!

What We're Looking For

  • Willing to learn and follow food prep procedures
  • Punctual, hardworking, and team player
  • Can work mornings (shift starts 5:30 AM)
  • Food handling or kitchen experience is a plus (not required)
  • Zamboanga City resident preferred

Cashier / Front-of-House Staff

Full-Time On-Site Talon-Talon, ZC

You'll be the friendly face of Charito's — taking orders, handling transactions, and making sure every customer leaves happy. A smile and a positive attitude are the most important qualifications!

What We're Looking For

  • Friendly and customer-service oriented
  • Basic math and cash handling skills
  • Organized and attentive to detail
  • Can communicate clearly in Filipino and/or English
  • Experience in retail or F&B is a plus

Delivery Rider

Part-Time Field Work Zamboanga City

Help us deliver fresh, hot food directly to our customers around Zamboanga City. Must have your own motorcycle and a valid driver's license.

What We're Looking For

  • Own motorcycle and valid driver's license
  • Familiar with Zamboanga City roads and barangays
  • Reliable, punctual, and responsible
  • Has a smartphone for route navigation
  • Willing to work flexible hours

Social Media & Marketing Assistant

Part-Time Hybrid Zamboanga City

Help us grow Charito's online presence! You'll create content, manage our Facebook and Instagram pages, and engage with our customers. Perfect for a creative student or recent graduate.

What We're Looking For

  • Familiar with Facebook, Instagram, and content creation
  • Basic photo/video editing skills (Canva, CapCut, etc.)
  • Good written communication in Filipino and English
  • Creative, proactive, and self-motivated
  • Marketing, Comms, or related course background is a plus
